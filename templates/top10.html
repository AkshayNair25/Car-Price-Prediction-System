Top 10 Used Cars to Buy (2021)

  • 1. Maruti Alto 800

    The first car in our list of Best Second-hand Cars to Buy in India is the Maruti Alto 800, which is also among the cheapest cars in India. The entry-level model impresses with its low fuel consumption, reliable nature and good ride quality. These are the very reasons it’s so popular in the second-hand car market.

    Price Range : ₹ 1,73,000 - ₹ 2,54,000

  • 2. Maruti Swift

    The latest generation Maruti Swift was introduced in India in February last year and has been quick to become one of the highest selling cars in the country. It has got funky looks, powerful yet economical engine options, both manual and automatic gearshift choices, and low-cost spares.

    Price Range : ₹ 78,900 - ₹ 3,25,900

    While Volkswagen cars don’t enjoy a huge popularity in new or used car market, the Polo is somewhat of an exception. This is only because of its good build quality, reliable engines, well-appointed cabin and great dynamics.

    Price Range : ₹ 1,20,000 - ₹ 5,93,000

  • 5. Maruti Dzire

    The Maruti Dzire has been among the highest selling cars in India. The high demand for the Swift-based sedan is owing to the sufficiently spacious cabin, economical engines, plush ride quality and low cost of maintenance. The high demand for the Dzire makes it one of the most popular models in the second-hand car market.

    Price Range : ₹ 5,68,000 - ₹ 6,10,000

  • 6. Maruti Ciaz

    The Maruti Ciaz is spacious and highly comfortable. It is even quite well-equipped and has been available in multiple variants to suit all sorts of budgets. It’s easy to maintain and is among the most economical cars in its class. All this translates to a really high popularity in the used car markets.

    Price Range : ₹ 5,75,000 - ₹ 10,50,000

  • 7. Honda City

  • 9. Toyota Innova
